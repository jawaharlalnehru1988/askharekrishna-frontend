Sri Ramayanam

Dive into the eternal epic of duty, devotion, and righteousness. Listen to the sacred Kandas (chapters) that chronicle the divine journey of Lord Rama, Sita Devi, Lakshmana, and Hanuman.

Life in the Dandaka forest, the encounter with Surpanakha, the golden deer illusion, and the tragic abduction of Sita by Ravana.
